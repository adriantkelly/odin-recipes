Chicken Enchilada Recipe

A Warm Dish of Chicken Enchiladas That Will Melt In Your Mouth

A warm dish of chicken enchiladas. Warm chicken, enchilada sauce, and gooey cheese topped with green onion. Best served with your favorite side of spanish rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cilantro
  • Lime Juice
  • Enchilada Sauce
  • Green Chiles
  • Corn
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook and shred your chicken
  2. Combine in a large mixing bowl 1/4 cup of enchilada sauce, corn, green chiles and chopped cilantro
  3. Spoon out mixture evenly into 8 large flour tortillas
  4. Wrap tortillas and place seam side down in a greased baking pan
  5. Pour remaining enchilada sauce over the enchiladas and spread evenly
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 350F for 30 mins
  7. Remove from oven and top with shredded cheese
  8. Place back in oven for 5 mins to melt cheese
  9. Allow to cool for 5-10 mins then garnish with green onion and cilantro
  10. Serve on a plate with side of rice
