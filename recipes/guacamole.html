Guacamole Recipe

Creamy Guacamole That Leaves You Wanting More

A nice creamy guacamole made with fresh avocados, lime juice, tomatoes, red onion and spiced to perfection

Ingredients

  • Ripe avocados
  • Lime Juice
  • Cilantro
  • Red Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Flaky Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Score each avocado half and scoop into a large bowl
  2. Dice red onion and combine with avocado
  3. Dice tomatoes and add to bowl
  4. Chop cilantro and add to bowl
  5. Add seasonings and stir
  6. Add fresh lime juice and combine to taste
  7. Serve in a large bowl with a tortilla chips to scoop
